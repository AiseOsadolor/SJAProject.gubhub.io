First Aid Training & Certification

We provide comprehensive first aid training and certification for individuals, schools, organisations, and communities. Our courses equip participants with essential lifesaving skills to respond effectively to emergencies at home, work, or in public spaces.

Emergency Medical Response

Our trained volunteers and professionals offer emergency medical services at public events, during disasters, and in times of crisis. We provide rapid response, first aid, and support to those in need, helping to save lives and reduce suffering.

Community Health Outreach

We organise health education campaigns, free medical screenings, and outreach programmes to promote healthy living and disease prevention in communities across Nigeria.

Youth Development Programmes

Through our Cadet and Youth programmes, we empower young people with leadership, first aid, and life skills, preparing them to be responsible citizens and future leaders.

Event Medical Coverage

We provide professional first aid and ambulance services at sporting events, concerts, religious gatherings, and other public functions, ensuring the safety and wellbeing of all participants.

Partnerships & Advocacy

We collaborate with government agencies, NGOs, and private organisations to strengthen emergency response systems, advocate for public health, and build resilient communities.

Volunteering & Capacity Building

We offer opportunities for individuals to volunteer, develop new skills, and make a positive impact in their communities through our various programmes and activities.

Get Involved

Join us in our mission to save lives and promote health in Nigeria. Contact us to learn how you can participate, partner, or support our work.
