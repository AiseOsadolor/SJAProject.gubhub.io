Brief History

St John Ambulance Nigeria is a voluntary humanitarian organisation and part of the global St John Ambulance movement. Established in Nigeria in 1959, it is dedicated to the teaching and practice of first aid, emergency response, and community health services across the country.

  • Community Health Outreach
