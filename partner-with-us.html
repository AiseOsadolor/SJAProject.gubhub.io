Why Partner With St John Ambulance Nigeria?

At St John Ambulance Nigeria, we believe that collaboration is key to building resilient communities and saving lives. By partnering with us, your organisation can make a lasting impact on health, safety, and emergency response across Nigeria.

Partnership Opportunities

  • Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR): Support our first aid training, community outreach, and emergency response programmes as part of your CSR initiatives.
  • Training Partnerships: Arrange first aid and emergency preparedness training for your staff, students, or community members.
  • Event Medical Coverage: Collaborate with us to provide professional first aid and ambulance services at your events.
  • Joint Health Campaigns: Work with us on health education, disease prevention, and wellness campaigns.
  • Resource Support: Donate equipment, supplies, or funds to strengthen our capacity to serve communities.
  • Volunteer Engagement: Encourage your employees or members to volunteer with St John Ambulance Nigeria.

Benefits of Partnership

  • Contribute to saving lives and improving health in Nigeria
  • Enhance your organisation’s social impact and reputation
  • Access to first aid and emergency response training for your team
  • Recognition on our platforms and at events
  • Opportunities for joint advocacy and community engagement
